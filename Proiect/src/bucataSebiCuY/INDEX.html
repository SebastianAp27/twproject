Romania - Female obesity prevalence as a share of female ages 18+
In 2016, female obesity prevalence for Romania was 21.6 %. Between 1997 and 2016, female obesity prevalence of Romania grew substantially from 16.6 to 21.6 % rising at an increasing annual rate that reached a maximum of 1.91% in 2015 and then decreased to 1.41% in 2016.
